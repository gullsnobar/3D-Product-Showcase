Frequently Asked Questions

What is your return policy? +

We offer a 30-day return policy for all products. Items must be in original condition with all accessories and packaging. Free return shipping is provided for defective items.

How long is the warranty? +

All NEXUS products come with a 2-year manufacturer warranty covering defects in materials and workmanship. Extended warranty options are available for purchase.

Do you ship internationally? +

Yes, we ship to over 50 countries worldwide. International shipping rates and delivery times vary by location. Customs duties and taxes are the responsibility of the customer.

How can I track my order? +

Once your order ships, you'll receive a tracking number via email. You can track your package on our website or directly through the carrier's tracking system.

Are your products compatible with all devices? +

Our products are designed to work with most modern devices that support Bluetooth 5.0 or higher. Check individual product specifications for detailed compatibility information.
